0:40 [User] struggles to [goal] because [root cause]…
Problem

SMB founders struggle to scale support
because agents have no operational layer.

No inboxAgents have nowhere to receive workEvery function runs through email. Agents can reason but have no persistent address to receive, triage, or act on that work.
No coordinationAgents can't hand off to each otherSupport needs billing to confirm a payment. Without a shared layer, every handoff falls back to a human.
No actionNo scoped permissions to act autonomouslyEven when an agent knows the answer, there's no framework — no rules, no tools, no audit trail.
⚡Agents are capable. What's missing is the operational layer that lets them receive, coordinate, and act.
